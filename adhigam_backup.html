About Adhigam

Empowering Knowledge, Building Communities

Our Vision

We envision a community that is empowered, self-sustaining, and resilient—thriving through its own resources and ingenuity. By nurturing independence and long-term growth, we aim to inspire individuals to take ownership of their futures and become catalysts for enduring, positive transformation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a self-reliant, forward-thinking community by equipping individuals with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to fuel sustainable growth. We champion resilience, spark innovation, and foster collaborative spirit.

Our Values - TATVAS

Transparency Accountability Trust Vision Action Sustainability

Our Programs

Empowering youth, women, and children through innovative education and skill development

Creative Learning & Skill-Building

Ages 4–17 years

Weekly engagements using fun games, storytelling, and art & craft activities aimed at developing critical thinking, collaboration, and self-expression.

  • Fun games and activities
  • Storytelling sessions
  • Art & craft workshops
  • Critical thinking development

Kathak Dance Group

All ages welcome

A small but enthusiastic group exploring classical dance as a way to connect with culture and build confidence, discipline, and joy.

  • Classical dance training
  • Cultural connection
  • Confidence building
  • Performance opportunities

Theatre Group

Ages 8–17 years

Focused on performance, roleplay, and storytelling to help children explore emotions, social issues, and communication.

  • Performance training
  • Roleplay activities
  • Storytelling workshops
  • Social issue awareness
